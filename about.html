About Me

aash-raa-taass-lim/

I'm Ashra Taslima, a UI/UX designer and Full Stack Developer with a deep-rooted passion for art that fuels my creative approach to designing user-centered digital experiences. With over six years of experience, I blend my love for artistic expression— inspired by the fluidity of watercolors and the vibrancy of visual storytelling—with technical expertise in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and React. Whether crafting intuitive wireframes or prototyping responsive interfaces, I draw on my artistic sensibility to create visually compelling designs that resonate with users. My work at Find A Lawyer, where I boosted funding by 43% through user-focused design, and at Credit One Bank, where I enhanced efficiency by 85% with a custom CRM tool, reflects my commitment to merging art and technology. For me, every pixel is a brushstroke, transforming ideas into impactful, accessible, and engaging digital canvases.
